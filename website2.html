Biography

Pengsu Jagger Yang

Hello my name is Pengsu Yang. I am a 5'7 libero that plays on the Clovis High School Boys volleyball team. I am a 4 year varsity player and achieved many awards such as placing 1st at Granite Bay tournament, 1st at San Luis Obispo tournament, 2nd in CIF, and 2nd in the northern section of the state. I played for Tsunami Volleyball Club which we also placed 3rd in NCVA. I have my stats provided in the "About" section with all of stats in the recent 2 years. As well I have provided recent film from my highschool season this year in the "Film" section.

Future

After high school I plan to attend Fresno State as I will be majoring in computer science. My high school computer science teacher, Mr. Maresh has really helped me strive for my passion of computer science. As for volleyball I will not be playing for college but will be involved in the Hmong volleyball community around the world.
